RETURN  AND  WARRANTY  POLICIES   1.  Frames  may  be  returned  within  30  days  of  purchase  2.  If  you  cannot  adapt  to  a  no-lined  bifocal  we  will  remake  into  a   lined
bifocal or single vision at no charge within 90 days 3.  If  you  decide  you  are  unhappy  with  a  frame  we  are  bound  by  the
policies of your insurance company in regard to return and or discounts.

If your insurance does not allow for a frame change we will
allow a 30% discount on lenses or use your second pair benefit
if there is one.
If no insurance is used we will allow a one time remake
within 30 days or original order.
NO REFUNDS WILL BE MADE ON LENSES MADE TO THE DRS WRITTEN
PRESCRIPTION .
We will refund the frame portion within 30 days.

4.  Frames  will  be  covered  under  a  one  time  manufacturers  warranty  if  they
should break.
What constitutes a manufacturer's warranty differs by company.
Please bring all broken pieces in when you are warranting your frame.
if any super glue or animal bites are present the warranty is
voided.
5.  We  do  adjustments  to  customers  frames  no  matter  where  they  were
purchased.

If a customer's frame is to break during adjustment pal optical will
offer you a 20% discount off a new frame as a courtesy.
6.  There  is  no  warranty  on  lenses  purchased  unless  you  purchased  a
premium scratch warranty(td2) or an anti reflective coating which provides a coating
warranty.
Please be advised any scratches that go deeper than the coating will
not come out if the coating is reapplied.

In no circumstances will lenses be remade for free if scratches are
done while in the customers possession outside of the above cases.
7.  If  a   doctor  makes  a  correction  or  change  to  your  prescription  within
45 days of the original order, we will remake your lenses one
time according to the terms of your insurance if insurance was used.
In no circumstances will lenses be refunded that have been made to
the doctor's prescription.

REMEMBER  THAT  YOU  ARE  A  PRIORITY  TO  PAL  OPTICAL  AND  WE  WILL  DO
EVERYTHING WITHIN REASON TO ADDRESS ANY CONCERN THAT ARISES TO THE BEST
OF OUT ABILITY!
