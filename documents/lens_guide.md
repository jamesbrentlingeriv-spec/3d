# Lens Guide & Customization Options

Pal Optical's on-site lab offers a complete range of prescription lens types, materials, and coatings tailored to your lifestyle and prescription. Below is an overview of the options you can order:

## 1. Lens Types
* **Single Vision**: Designed to correct vision at a single distance (either near/reading, intermediate/computer, or distance).
* **Bifocals**: Feature two distinct viewing zones (distance on top, reading on the bottom) separated by a visible segment line.
* **Progressive Lenses (No-Line Bifocals)**: Provide a smooth, line-free transition from distance vision at the top, intermediate vision in the middle, to near vision at the bottom.

## 2. Lens Materials & Upgrades
* **Polycarbonate**: Light, impact-resistant lenses ideal for active lifestyles, safety glasses, and children's eyewear.
* **High-Index**: Ultra-thin and lightweight lenses designed specifically for strong prescriptions, reducing the "coke-bottle" thickness effect.

## 3. Premium Coatings & Treatments
* **Anti-Reflective (AR) Coating**: Reduces glare, night-driving reflections, and digital eye strain while improving lens transparency and appearance.
* **Blue-Light Filtering**: Filters out high-energy visible (HEV) blue light emitted by digital screens and LED lighting to help reduce eye fatigue.
* **Transitions & Photochromics**: Light-intelligent lenses that stay completely clear indoors and darken automatically when exposed to outdoor UV sunlight.
